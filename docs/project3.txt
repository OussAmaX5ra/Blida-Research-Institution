Project 3: Academic Research Lab Institutional Site

Main Focus: Create new Research Teams and Publications:

   A. Research Teams (The "Units"): This feature allows the laboratory to organize its
      members into specific scientific divisions.

   •   Team Identity: A unique name for the research group and its core mission or
       "Research Focus".

   •   Leadership: The name of the Professor or Senior Researcher leading the team.

   •   Member List: A multi-select or list area to add Professors, Doctors, and PhD
       students belonging to that unit.

   •   Active Projects: A field to list current scientific projects the team is
       investigating.

   B. Publications (The "Scientific Output"): This is a functional database where the
      lab tracks its scholarly contributions to journals and conferences.

   •   Document Info: The title of the research paper and the full list of contributing
       authors.

   •   Publisher Details: The name of the Journal or Conference where the work was
       presented and the year of publication.

   •   Access Link: A URL (PDF_Link) pointing to the digital version of the paper for
       visitors to read.

   •   Categorization: A tag system to link the publication to a specific Research Team
       or scientific theme.

1. Updated Mind Map (Conception)

   •   Laboratory Identity: Mission, vision, and core research axes.

   •   Teams & Members: Hierarchical view of Research Teams, Faculty, and PhD
       students.

   •   Management Portal : A restricted or specialized area where an admin can add a
       new Research Team to the lab, or a new publication to a team.

   •   Research Output: Digital library of projects and publications.

   •   News & Gallery: Live feed of lab news and media library.

2. Updated Functional Requirements (The MERN Way)

   •   Database (MongoDB):
          o   Team Collection: Updated fields to include TeamName, LeaderName,
              ResearchFocus, and an array for TeamMembers (Professors, Doctors, and
              PhD Students).

          o   Publication Collection: Title, Authors, Year, Publisher, and PDF_Link.

          o   News Collection: Headline, Date, Image, and FullStory.

   •   Backend (Node/Express):

          o   POST /api/teams (New): A route to receive team data from the frontend
              and save it to MongoDB.

          o   Search API: /api/publications/search?query=AI for specific research
              papers.

          o   Member API: Fetch members grouped by their Research Team.

   •   Frontend (React):

          o   Team Creation Form (New): A dedicated page or modal with input fields
              for the new team's name, leader, and focus area (The same for a new
              publication).

          o   Search Bar: Dynamic search component for filtering publications.

          o   Team Grid: Responsive layout showing team cards with "Click to see all
              members" functionality.

3. Non-Functional & Innovative Requirements

   •   Modern UI: High-performance and Responsive design (mobile-first) for the
       gallery and publication list.

   •   Innovation (The "Pro" Touch):

          o   Citation Exporter: A button on each publication card generating BibTeX or
              APA citations.

          o   PhD Progress Tracker: A visual timeline showing the status of ongoing
              research projects.
